Data Transmission

Overview

Sensor-enhanced applications may collect increasingly large amounts of data from their users. We implemented three initial policies for data transfer. The first two are, simply: do not transfer (data is only stored on the phone), and transfer immediately. However, you may not need real-time access to collected data, and transmitting data over 3G connections may bear a cost on users, we included a means for asynchronous data transfer. More specifically, the library waits for the phone to connect to Wi-Fi and transmits the data; should a Wi-Fi connection not appear in another configurable timespan (by default, above 30 hours), then data is transmitted by any connection available.
